About Me.

UI/UX Designer & Developer

I first developed my design skills by teaching myself UI/UX design and front-end coding as a founder of a mobile app development company. In order to further advance my skills, I honed and validated my design talents through the User Experience Design Immersive training at General Assembly. Since General Assembly I've been working freelance and with a small agency on dozens of different products.

I've worked on a multitude of projects that range from small UI design changes to entire site redesigns and implementations. I have experience with user research and testing, designing the UI/UX of responsive web/mobile apps, and working with HTML/CSS and bootstrap to implement designs. I've worked on several e-commerce sites (mostly on Shopify or self-developed sites) through which I've become very comfortable with Liquid and Google analytics (tracking e-commerce funnels). To sum up; I'm a problem solver who will do whatever it takes to get a job done.
